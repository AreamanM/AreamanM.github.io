What is the wall?

The wall is microblog; a place to archive thoughts and ideas that you want to preserve, perhaps to look back at in the future or even to share with others at a later time. It is not intended to be a social network but rather a time machine into your mind in the past.

My motivation for creating the wall

My motivation for creating the wall stems from my enthusiasm to write about ideas that I find interesting. I often do not find the motivation to turn those short snippets in my mind into full fledged written pieces yet I still want to record them somewhere that isn't just the notes app on my phone.
